Introduction

Proof Logger ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our communication logging and verification service.

By using Proof Logger, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Account Information: Username, email address, and encrypted password
  • Communication Logs: Details of communications you choose to log, including method, recipient, description, and timestamps
  • File Attachments: Documents, images, audio recordings, and other files you upload
  • Authentication Data: Two-factor authentication settings and backup codes

Technical Information

  • Usage Data: Information about how you use our service, including access times and features used
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, and device identifiers
  • Security Logs: Authentication attempts, security events, and audit trail information
  • Verification Data: Cryptographic hashes, blockchain timestamps, and verification certificates

Automatically Collected Information

  • Cookies and Tracking: Session cookies for authentication and security
  • Analytics: Aggregated usage statistics to improve our service
  • Error Logs: Technical information to diagnose and fix issues

How We Use Your Information

Service Provision

  • Create and maintain your communication logs
  • Generate cryptographic verification hashes
  • Create blockchain timestamps for immutable proof
  • Provide public verification services
  • Enable search and organization features

Security and Authentication

  • Authenticate your identity and maintain account security
  • Detect and prevent unauthorized access
  • Monitor for suspicious activity and security threats
  • Maintain audit logs for security purposes

Service Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve functionality
  • Develop new features and capabilities
  • Optimize performance and reliability
  • Provide customer support and technical assistance
